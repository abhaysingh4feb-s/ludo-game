## Overview

Ludo is an offline board game for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This app does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Information We Collect

**None.** This app does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or telemetry. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no user profiles.

## Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **Game saves** — to resume interrupted games
- **Player statistics** — wins, kills, games played (stored by player name you enter)
- **User preferences** — selected theme, sound settings, AI difficulty

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted over the internet, not synced to any cloud service, and not accessible to any third party.

## Third-Party Services

The app uses the **Google Fonts** package which may download font files from Google's servers on first launch. This downloads only font data — no personal information is collected or transmitted. No other third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools are used.

## Internet Access

This app is designed to work **100% offline**. No internet connection is required to play. The app does not request network permissions.

## Children's Privacy (COPPA)

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users. Since no data is collected at all, this app is fully compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and similar regulations.

## Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time:

1. Go to **Android Settings**
2. Tap **Apps** → **Ludo**
3. Tap **Storage** → **Clear Data**

This removes all game saves, statistics, and preferences.

## Permissions

This app requires **no special permissions**. It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other device features.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be included in the app update. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated accordingly.
